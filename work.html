My musical journey

Throughout my musical journey, I've embraced the saxophone's soulful melodies in both tenor and alto registers as a vital member of my school's brass band. Transitioning to the rhythmic depths of the bass guitar, I lent my talents to Taxila Central College's stage band, setting the pulse for countless performances. Now, as a dedicated member of the faculty music band, "Fit-Tunes," at the University of Moratuwa's Faculty of Information Technology, I proudly hold the mantle of permanent bass guitarist, anchoring our harmonious explorations with steadfast rhythm and passion.

First Year Project

Our project makes tea in a cool new way! We use special tech stuff like Arduino and sensors to control everything. First, we get fresh tea leaves from small farms. Then, we blend them with water and heat it up until it boils. The steam goes through a coil to make it super pure. If you want, we can add yummy flavors like honey or cinnamon. Finally, we mix the pure tea with hot water for a delicious cup. Our project is all about making awesome tea while helping local farmers and being eco-friendly.
